# ARCHITECTURE: TETHERING PROTOCOL (Handshake Rules)

**Objective**: Prevent "Ontological Drift" and "Narrative Collapse" by ensuring domain especialistas approve feature plans.

---

## 🔗 The Primary Tethers

### 1. Philologist [Philology] <-> Librarian [Evidence]
*   **Rule**: Reconstructed fragments MUST correlate with PDF research summary keywords.
*   **Approval**: All reconstruction mini-games in `ManuscriptView.tsx` must be verified by the Librarian.

### 2. Retroist [Mechanics] <-> Chronicler [Narrative]
*   **Rule**: Every mechanical "Stigma" or "Spirit" pulse must trigger a corresponding narrative consequence in the log or a scene.
*   **Approval**: All new `gameStore` stats must have a "Narrative Cost" defined by the Chronicler.

### 3. Cartographer [World] <-> Retroist [Naval Tactics]
*   **Rule**: Pursuer AI behavior is gated by the "Voyage Day" and "Current Coordinate."
*   **Approval**: Strategic move patterns (e.g. Venetian Ambushes) must be verified by the Cartographer.
